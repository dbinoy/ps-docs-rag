# BPRP Architecture Brief

Overview

Bill Payment Remittance Processing (BPRP) is Central 1's electronic payment consolidation system enabling business members (Billers) to aggregate and process payments through a lead financial institution.

Key Data Flows & Integration Points

  • Enrollment pipeline: Business member setup → Biller validation on Electronic Bill Payment System → BPRP arrangement activation
  • Service requests: Non-Central 1 institutions submit via prescribed channels; Central 1 institutions use direct submission
  • Settlement & tracing: BC/Ontario-specific; involves inter-institution fund transfers and reconciliation reporting
  • Data outputs: Two standard BPRP reports + configurable data files for BC/Ontario participants

Critical Design Constraints

1. Multi-institution architecture: Lead bank model with provincial central intermediation; non-Central 1 institutions operate under delegation agreements

2. Regulatory compliance mandatory: Financial institutions establish enrollment policies; legal review required per chapter 3

3. Provincial variance: Settlement, tracing, reports, and data file availability differ by province; architecture must support regional branching logic

4. AFT dependency: Relies on Automated Funds Transfer authorization framework for debits/credits

Pre-Design Considerations

  • Confirm provincial feature scope before architecture decisions
  • Document lead bank vs. member institution role separation
  • Plan for CCIN (Corporate Client Identification Number) modification workflows
  • Design idempotent service request handling with audit trails for enrollment/modification/cancellation operations
