# CRA Business Taxes – Architecture Brief

Overview

CRA Business Taxes is a PaymentStream Direct application enabling authorized users to submit Canada Revenue Agency remittance vouchers and business tax payments through Central 1. Supports GST/HST, Excise, Taxation, and Customs Programs per CPA Rule G4.

Key Architectural Data Flows

1. Dual Workflow Options: Single workflow (front-line staff only) or multi-stage workflow with segregated roles

2. Payment Processing: Electronic submission of CRA business tax payments and filings; manual processing fallback for unsupported voucher types

3. Settlement: Direct settlement between Central 1 and member FIs (BC/Ontario specific rules; separate handling for other regions)

4. Reconciliation & Reporting: Mandatory reporting and reconciliation outputs for financial institutions

Critical Integration Points

  • User Management: Role-based access control tied to PaymentStream Direct authentication
  • Remittance Voucher Validation: Electronic vs. manual processing determination per voucher type
  • CRA File Submission: Direct integration with CRA acceptance protocols
  • Bundle Reconstruction: Handling for lost/unprocessable voucher bundles (mail/courier fallback)

Design Constraints

  • CPA Rule G4 compliance mandatory
  • Environment separation (test/production)
  • Regional variance (BC/Ontario vs. external FIs)
  • Settlement and reconciliation processes are region-dependent
