# Payment Solutions: Master Architecture Overview

What is Payment Solutions?

Payment Solutions is Central 1's integrated payment processing platform serving member credit unions across Canada. It orchestrates all electronic and cheque-based fund transfers, settlements, and reconciliations—from bill payments and AFT (Automated Funds Transfer) to clearing, deposits, and government-backed lending programs. The platform connects financial institutions to Central 1's settlement infrastructure, regulatory frameworks, and external partners (EDC, AAFC, provincial centrals, clearing houses), enabling members to originate, route, and reconcile payments at scale.

Core End-to-End Flows

1. AFT Settlement (Outgoing)

Member initiates PAD or Direct Deposit → File submission or PaymentStream UI entry → Central 1 validation and posting (dual-entry: originator account + offset) → Clearing house delivery → Settlement T+1 or per due date → Reconciliation via MERG reports.

2. Bill Payment Processing

Customer or business member selects biller → Originating FI validates against biller and institution limits → Payment submitted via file or eCommSwitch → Central 1 consolidation → T+1 settlement by 5:30 am PT → Tracer initiates OLT for status/disputes → Reversal if required.

3. Clearing and Deposit Settlement

Deposit items captured (branch/corporate/mobile) → Central 1 receives, images, and validates → Same-day credit to member account → Incoming clearing items processed and settled → ORS portal enables chargeback and reconciliation without physical retrieval.

4. Government Lending Cycle (AgriInvest / CEBA)

Member opens participant account; AAFC/EDC validates eligibility → Institution submits Activity Files or repayment data (semi-monthly windows) within three business days → Central 1 posts to current account → Settlement advices generated → Institution reconciles via CAS Online or automated OREC matching.

5. User Onboarding & Wire Approval

Institution appoints Security Officers → Officers create Client Centre users (batch or individual) → Users register passwords and provision 2-step tokens (hardware or soft) → Upon wire initiation, user performs step-up authentication → Wire approval and settlement with audit trail.

---

Platform-Wide Integration Points

  • Client Centre Portal: Primary identity provider and session manager; downstream applications delegate authentication.
  • ServiceNow: Hard token ordering, user requests, CEBA/AgriInvest administration submissions.
  • File Exchange (SFTP + Web UI): Bidirectional file transport supporting AFT, bill payment, clearing reports, and reconciliation data.
  • Settlement System (GL Posting): Central 1 internal engine posting all transactions, offsets, fees, and adjustments to member current accounts.
  • CAS Online: Real-time balance and transaction queries; Account Group–based role filtering; 12-month history and statement delivery.
  • External Partners: EDC (CEBA funding), AAFC (AgriInvest eligibility), provincial centrals (BPRP routing), clearing houses (cheque exchange), eCommSwitch (bill payment alternative channel).
  • PaymentStream Platform: Modern UI/API layer for real-time AFT entry, CRA filing, and future payment origination extensibility.
  • Regulatory Framework: CPA rules, FINTRAC compliance, provincial legislation (BC vs. Ontario), dispute/recourse procedures.
