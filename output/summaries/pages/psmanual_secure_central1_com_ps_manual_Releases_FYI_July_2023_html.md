# FYI – July 2023 Summary

- **Purpose**: Documents July 2023 updates to Payment Solutions Manual procedures, primarily affecting form submission workflows for Canadian credit unions by province.

- **Key Process Change**: Saskatchewan, Manitoba, and Alberta credit unions now submit CPS Admin forms and related requests directly to Central 1 via ServiceNow ticket attachments or email instead of routing through provincial central organizations—represents shift from distributed to centralized submission model.

- **Forms Updated** (all revised):
  - CPS Admin Appointment Certificate (1918)
  - CPS Admin Modification/Deletion Request (2035)
  - Direct Services for Business – Financial Institution Administrator Creation/Change/Deletion Request (2169)
  - FTP User Request (2010)

- **Integration Dependencies**: ServiceNow ticketing system is now a primary submission channel for form intake; email submission (support@central1.com) serves as alternative pathway—both routes feed into Central 1 processing workflows.

- **Terminology Harmonization**: References updated from "secure site" to "Client Centre" and "Credit Union" to "Financial Institution" across documentation, indicating broader terminology standardization initiative.

- **Architectural Constraint**: Form submissions now require attachment handling capability in ServiceNow integration; no indication of API-driven submission or validation workflows—suggest document-based processing model.

- **Support Contact**: Single point of escalation: Central 1 Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878 Option 1, support@central1.com).
