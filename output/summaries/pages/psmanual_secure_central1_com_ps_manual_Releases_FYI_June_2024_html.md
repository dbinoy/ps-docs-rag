# FYI – June 2024 Summary

- **Purpose**: June 2024 documentation updates to Payment Solutions Manual across multiple operational volumes addressing user management and system access clarifications.

- **AFT Volume Changes**: PaymentStream AFT Part (Section 6.3) clarified that CPS Admin Security Officers must use the User Management system—not alternative methods—for managing business member users within AFT origination workflows.

- **CAS Online Access Constraint**: Current Account Services Online cannot be provisioned through User Management; CAS Online access requires alternative administration procedures separate from standard user management flows.

- **ORS Username Restrictions**: Online Return System (ORS) usernames do not support special characters; this constraint is enforced at username creation and documented in both Access & Administration Volume (Section 9.14) and Clearing & Deposits Volume (Section 5.2), indicating a single validation rule across ORS interfaces.

- **User Management System Centralization**: User Management acts as the primary provisioning control point for most Payment Solutions components (AFT, ORS) but with documented exceptions (CAS Online), suggesting a hub-and-spoke access control architecture.

- **Multi-Volume Consistency**: Duplicate clarifications across volumes (ORS special character rules) indicate tight coupling between Clearing & Deposits and Access & Administration operational domains.
