# FYI – May 2025 Release Summary

- **Purpose**: Documents May 2025 updates to the Payment Solutions Manual, spanning Central 1 Banking Services and Document Library components.

- **Treasury Connect Integration**: Authorized Treasury Connect users can now transfer funds between Central 1 accounts of identical currency via Treasury Connect—represents new inter-account transfer capability that architects must account for in permission/authorization models.

- **Central 1 Current Accounts Module**: Treasury Connect User Guide added to document library (Section 1.9); implies Treasury Connect is a critical reference system for current accounts operations and dependency management should be established.

- **Inter-Branch & Corporate Client Transfers**: Section 4.1 updated with transfer authorization rules—architects should review scope of "same currency" constraint and whether multi-currency transfers require distinct logic paths or system integrations.

- **Document Management System Change**: Payee's PAD Agreement (form 1696) now includes e-signature fields with enhanced accessibility standards—indicates evolution toward digital-first workflows; architects should verify backend validation rules for e-signature metadata and audit trail requirements.

- **Support & Escalation**: Central 1 Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878, Option 1) is the primary escalation path for questions regarding these changes and should be documented in runbook procedures.
