# FYI – October 2023 Summary

- **Purpose**: October 2023 release notes documenting updates across Payment Solutions Manual covering bill payments, clearing/deposits, and electronic transactions modules.

- **Terminology Migration**: Systematic replacement of "secure site" references with "Client Centre" terminology across all affected parts, including revised definitions and updated access instructions—indicates UI/portal naming convention change with corresponding documentation updates required for all client-facing systems.

- **Bill Payments Changes**: Daily automated removal of inactive member biller accounts in Electronic Bill Payments System; architects must account for account lifecycle management and potential data retention/archival requirements for inactive accounts.

- **Clearing & Deposits Coverage**: Updates span multiple clearing item types (cheque imaging, money orders, FX drafts, ICBC deposits, bonds, provincial concentrator accounts)—indicates interconnected clearing subsystems with shared Client Centre access patterns.

- **Electronic Transactions Scope**: Updates to PaymentStream™ Direct, Interac e-Transfer limits/settlement/tracing/reporting, international transfers, and Interac e-Transfer OAS—suggests settlement and reporting data flows require Client Centre integration points.

- **Form 2117 Business Rule**: Cash parcel discrepancy claims now require 24-hour verification window post-receipt and must be submitted exclusively via armoured car delivery to RBC—enforces temporal constraint and physical delivery dependency in cash reconciliation workflows.

- **Support & Governance**: Issues escalate to Central 1 Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878 or support@central1.com); indicates external dependency on Central 1 for operational questions during implementation.
