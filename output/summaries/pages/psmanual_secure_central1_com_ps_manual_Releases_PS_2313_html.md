# PS 23:13 Release Summary (June 20, 2023)

- **Release scope**: Documentation restructuring and content consolidation for CRA (Canada Revenue Agency) Business Taxes payment processing module, incorporating Form 5915 procedures with updated formatting standards and reorganized information architecture.

- **Key structural changes**: Four chapters reorganized—Legal/Policy consolidated into Chapter 3; "Internal Workflows" renamed to Chapter 4; "Identifying Remittance Vouchers for Electronic Processing" renamed to Chapter 6; Manual remittance processing elevated to dedicated Chapter 10.

- **Core process flows affected**: Bill Payment Reversal Request workflow (Chapter 9) and remittance voucher identification/processing workflows (Chapters 6, 10) now have standardized procedures with updated visual documentation.

- **Contact and fraud handling integration point**: Section 1.4 added formal procedures for CRA contact regarding bad actors, identity theft, and fraudulent payment detection—suggests integration with fraud detection/reporting workflows and external CRA escalation channels.

- **Deprecation**: Business Taxes User Guide, Form 5915 marked **Obsolete** in Document Library—dependent systems/processes must migrate from Form 5915 to consolidated procedures in the updated CRA Business Taxes Part.

- **No data model or field-level changes documented**: Release focuses on procedural reorganization rather than schema modifications, API changes, or remittance voucher format updates.

- **Support dependencies**: Client escalations route through Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878 Option 1 or support@central1.com)—critical for post-release documentation feedback loops.
