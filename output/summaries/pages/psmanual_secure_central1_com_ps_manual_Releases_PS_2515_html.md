# Summary: Payment Services Class A Fee Schedule Update (v25:15)

- **Subject**: Update to Form 2725 (Payment Services Class A Fee Schedule) effective June 30, 2025, adding a new passthrough fee for debit channel fraud monitoring.

- **Scope & Eligibility**: Form 2725 applies exclusively to Class A credit unions in British Columbia and Ontario; updates do not affect other customer segments or provinces.

- **Fee Addition**: Section 4.1 (Debit Operations Fee) now includes "PRM Debit Channel Fraud Monitoring Service"—a passthrough fee sourced from Everlink, requiring revenue flow-through without margin or markup.

- **Client Notification Timeline**: Affected clients received advance notice in April 2025; implementation occurred by June 30, 2025 cutoff, indicating a 2-month notification-to-deployment window.

- **Integration Dependency**: Everlink is an external fraud monitoring service provider; fee structure and changes are downstream from Everlink's pricing, creating a vendor management dependency requiring change tracking and communication protocols.

- **Document Status**: Form 2725 is marked "Revised"—architects should verify current version control and ensure billing systems, APIs, and fee calculation engines reference the updated document baseline to prevent dual-rate scenarios.

- **Support & Escalation**: Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878, Option 1 or support@central1.com) owns inquiry routing; architects should ensure escalation paths exist for fee-related integration issues.
