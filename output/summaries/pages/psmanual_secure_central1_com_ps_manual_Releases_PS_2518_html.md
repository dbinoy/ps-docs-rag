# Payment Solutions Release 25:18 Summary

- **Overview**: Release update (August 12, 2025) documenting procedural changes for Receiver General Concentrator account deposit management within the Payment Solutions Manual.

- **Key Change**: New "Receiver General Concentrator Account Deposits" Part added to the Clearing and Deposits Volume, replacing obsolete Form 5976 (Branch Capture – Processing Receiver General Deposits for Central 1's Concentrator Account).

- **Process Scope**: Covers deposit procedures for Receiver General funds flowing through Central 1's Concentrator Account—a critical clearing and settlement touchpoint requiring updated operational guidance.

- **Documentation Artifact**: Form 5976 is now obsolete; architects should reference the new dedicated Part in the Clearing and Deposits Volume rather than legacy form-based procedures when designing or modifying deposit workflows.

- **Integration Dependency**: Changes likely impact branch-level capture systems, clearing house reconciliation processes, and concentrator account ledger reconciliation—verify downstream system alignment with new procedures before deployment.

- **Support/Governance**: Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878, Option 1) and support@central1.com are the change authority; escalate procedural clarifications or implementation conflicts to this contact.

- **Architect Action**: Review the full Receiver General Concentrator Account Deposits Part content to identify schema, validation rules, and data flow changes affecting deposit submissions, clearing settlement, or reporting interfaces.
