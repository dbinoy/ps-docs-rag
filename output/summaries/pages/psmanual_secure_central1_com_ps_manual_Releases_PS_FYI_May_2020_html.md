# FYI – May 2020 Summary

- **Purpose**: Release notes documenting updates to the Payment Solutions Manual for May 2020, specifically related to bond handling and deposit processes.

- **Primary Change**: Section 4.6 (Handling Non-Certified Instruments) clarified regulatory status of Canada Savings Bonds in Payroll Savings Plans—bonds no longer available for new purchases, but existing plan funds remain guaranteed with continued interest accrual until maturity or redemption.

- **Affected Process**: Redeeming or Transferring Bonds workflow (renamed from prior "Redeeming, Transferring, or Selling Bonds" nomenclature)—suggests constraints on sell operations and simplification of available bond actions.

- **Data Integrity Consideration**: Government form images updated to 2019 versions from Government of Canada source—architectural systems must support versioned form templates and may require document management integration for regulatory compliance.

- **Key Constraint**: Payroll Savings Plan bond redemption/transfer logic must enforce that existing plans are read-only for interest accrual purposes; no new purchases permitted through this channel.

- **Documentation Domain**: Falls under "Clearing and Deposits Volume" subsystem, indicating this ties to cash settlement and deposit reconciliation workflows.

- **Support Integration**: Central 1 Client Support Services (1-888-889-7878, support@central1.com) is the escalation point for implementation questions regarding these policy changes.
