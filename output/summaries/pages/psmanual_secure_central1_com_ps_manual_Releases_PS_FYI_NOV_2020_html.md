# FYI – November 2020 Summary

- **Overview**: November 2020 release notes documenting updates to Payment Solutions Manual across Currency Volume and Electronic Transactions Volume sections.

- **Currency Volume Update**: Section 9.6 clarifies counterfeit bank note handling procedures—RBC cash parcels containing counterfeit notes may be returned via courier or armoured car transport only; mail transmission is prohibited due to security/compliance constraints.

- **Electronic Transactions Volume Update**: Section 8.8 adds procedural clarification that PaymentStream Direct CRA account management is distinct from Business Taxes product, indicating separate data models and processing logic within the PaymentStream Direct platform.

- **External Dependency**: Western Union FX Drafts functionality updated in response to FINTRAC regulatory changes affecting beneficiary address requirements—integration point for cross-border payment processing with compliance-driven field validation modifications.

- **Document Artifacts**: Two versioned user guides (9182, 9183) published for Western Union FX Drafts with Role-Based Access Control differentiation (CU Admin Users vs. Order Creator Users), suggesting granular permission models in the system architecture.

- **Support/Escalation**: Central 1 Client Support Services is the integration point for questions, indicating external service dependency for user-facing issues and potential SLA implications for architects designing support workflows.
